# Overview

**N**INJAS **I**s **N**ot **J**ust **A**nother **S**nake

NINJAS is a *snake-like* game. You (the player) are a ninja.
As a ninja, your goal is to defeat all the samurai.
Some samurai are stronger than you.
They will chase you and try to defeat you.
Some samurai, on the other hand, are weaker than you.
They will run.
Catch the weaker samurai while avoiding the stronger samurai.
As you catch more and more samurai, your scarf will get longer.
This shows how strong you are!
*But*, make sure you don't trip.
It's easy to fall when you trip over something so long.

This project is here to give me experience in more programming languages.
This time, I'm learning TypeScript.
Additionally, this project is providing excellent practice with game design and development.
Eventually, this will also provide me with experience in artwork, designing sprites.
Additionally, I wanted something fun to make and fun to play.
Admittedly, while the idea for this formed out of the idea of making another snake-like, it also came from my desire to write a recursive acronym.
So here we are.
